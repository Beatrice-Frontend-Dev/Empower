FAQ

Will this affect my data?

No, keeping your financial information safe and secure remains
our highest priority, and we will continue to use the same
systems, encryption, and methodologies as before. For more
information, visit our security page.

Will the fees stay the same?

Yes. In addition to our tools remaining free, your wealth
management fees will remain unaffected.

Further Questions?

Please speak with your advisor or email us.

Will I have to change advisors?

Not unless you want to. Many clients have built strong
relationships with their financial advisors and we will do our best
to ensure they remain in place.

What else is changing?

While the products and services you've come to love have not
changed, some have new names:

Personal Capital Cash → Empower Personal Cash TM Personal Capital app → Empower Personal Dashboard app Daily Capital blog → The Currency blog

In addition, a number of Empower products and services will
become available to all Personal Capital customers and clients.
Check Empower.com for updates.
